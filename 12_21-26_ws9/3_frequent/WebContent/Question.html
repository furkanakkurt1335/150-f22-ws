Please read important information first.

Description

In this question, you are given a file with a body of text. You are expected to remove dots and commas from the text, and lower all letters in the text. Then, you need to count the occurrences of each word (text is split by split(' ')).

NOTE:If the occurrence of a word is less than 4, you need to remove those words from your counting structure.

The procedure you need to follow with the remaining words is as follows:

  1. Output the remaining words (sorted in reverse and then first 3 members left out) and their occurrence counts in separate lines, as seen below.
    • if there are 3 or fewer words remained, only output the 2nd task below.
  2. Output the resulting text as follows:
    • Only the first three occurrences, if there are more,
    • Without dots and commas,
    • All letters are lower-cased

For example: in file1.txt, when you sort the words and ignore words with occurrence counts less than 4, the first 3 words are value, the and target. We report occurrence counts without these three words.

At the end when we print the entire text back, we only use the first three occurrences of each word. We skip the fourth, fifth, ... occurrences. Please carefully examine the examples below:
