Workshop Question 6: Recursion - Even or Odd

Please read important information first.

Description

You are supposed to read two integers `n` (2 digit number) and `t` from a file. Then, you are expected to write a recursive function under the definition of `even_odd` given to you. The first even_odd number (at t=1) is the reverse of `n` if `n` is even, 11 more if `n` is odd. Even_odd numbers will go on until they become a three digit number. The `even_odd` function must return the `t`'th Even_odd number of `n`. Note: Using recursion is mandatory; other solutions are not accepted.
Input Output
file1.txt 
82
file2.txt 
69
file3.txt 
24
