Description

A person uses transportation to go to work or their school. This transportation system has personal cards that people use to pay for their trips. Students pay less money. The cards are capable of storing money, as well as providing a monthly service of 200 usages. In this monthly service, people pay a certain amount of money and they get 200 usages for the month. We want to calculate if they would be better off using the monthly service or just pay as they go, meaning they pay for each trip individually. This transportation system also allows secondary and tertiary (3) usages at discount prices. These discounts are for when somebody pays for the transportation system again in a couple hours from their last time. This is useful if somebody uses transportation and reaches their destination by more than 1 vehicle.

Price table in Yen
Type Student Worker
First payment 750 1500
Second payment 600 1200
Third payment 400 800
Monthly payment 17300 49310

You will be given an input file. This file's content is as follows: the first line is a string of 'Student' or 'Worker' indicating the person's card, the second line is for how many days a person uses the transportation system in a month (integer). Please assume, this person goes to their work or school in the morning and goes back home in the evening.

You are requested to output (print) as follows for all 3 cases of usage (1 vehicle, 2 vehicles or 3 vehicles): If they are better off using the monthly service. Print 'Monthly service' in the first line. Then, print how much money they save by using the monthly service instead of the pay-as-you-go system. On the other hand, if they are better off using the pay-as-you-go system, print 'Pay-as-you-go system' in the first line. Then, print how much money they save by using the pay-as-you-go system instead of the monthly service. The last print statement of the saved amount is in 'Main.py' already, so you don't have to write it. At the end, you print for all 3 cases of usage. First for if the person uses 1 vehicle to go to their workplace or school; second for if the person uses 2 vehicles; and third for if they use 3 vehicles.
